# Prologue - The Dream
[player] has a dream one day, walking through an empty subway tunnel, until they enter a building and see a ruined body on the ground with a symbol floating above it *(the symbol of Kill)*. The area flickers, the symbol attacking [player], and they wake up.

This location is where they will fight Kill at the end of the game.

# Act I - Life
The player goes through the intro level (their home), stopping to look through their belongings (if the player does so), finding a book about a story - the 7 powers, and learn the basic controls. They go get coffee, greeting people they know, then get on the subway, go to work, stop by the park on the way home, then get on the subway to go home.

That section is to get the player familiar with what the MC's world looks like.
After they get off the subway, a strange darkness fills the room, and they fall. The parts of their head breifly split into parts, each shining a different color, then they hit the ground.

# Act 2 - The Somn World
They wake up in a dark place that looks very similar to the Subway. Thinking they must have tripped and fallen asleep, they get up and go up the stairs - and find themselves in a completely different place. They travel around, alone for quite some time (doing some platforming levels), and soon they encounter a voice - introducing themselves as a Guide, someone who helps lost souls.

[Player] tells them that they are lost and confused, that they're trying to get home. Guide (the Pseudonym of Kill) tells them that they know a way, and that they can help. It takes 6 pieces to open they say, and that they are each held by tyrants, beings who selfishly keep them to themselves, keeping people trapped here.

They guide the player to the first town, then leave, stating that they will be there to guide them when needed. (Kill doesn't want anyone to recognize the voice of Kill).

The player assumes this must be a dream, with how familiar many things have looked, and how similar things are to the book that they read just hours ago.
They go through the town, seeing several people who look and act identical to the player's friends in the real world, who simply go by slightly different names. TOO coincidental, MUST be a dream.

...right?

They hear things from the townspeople about the first Bearer (That is what we'll call them), and the player embarks on their journey.

# Acts 3-7: The Bearers
The player goes to each location, fighting each Bearer and getting their powers, and subconciously helping people Speak, by helping them do things to have confidence, and to spare others rather than fight.

Each Bearer will often do some sort of challenge of sorts that the Player is asked to complete. Once they do, Kill takes over and attacks [player], then later uses that encounter to try to continue to convice the Player that the Bearers deserve to be beaten.

# Act 8: Climb
The Climb bearer is the last one - living high in the mountains. When asked for the power, they challenge the Player in a race to the top of the mountain, which the Player accepts. They race to the top of the mountain, and [player] wins. Climb is about to provide the power, before Kill takes over. They fight, Climb almost killing the Player, before the Player convinces them to stop. Speak, being FAR more powerful than Kill, forces Kill to leave. Climb explains some things relating to Kill, provides the power, then comments on how they didn't think anyone knew the art of Speak anymore, and thanks [player].

# Act 9: Kill.
The Player has lots of thoughts about things on their way to the final location, which guide claims is where they will find the lost power, Speak, the final one to open the gate home.

Just before they get there, Guide says that they need to go, and then doesn't respond to anything that [player] says.

[Player] continues, reaching a familiar looking stairway. They go down, and find themselves in the same place where they had their dream (in Act 1). They go down and find the body, where Guide speaks, revealing their plot, to have the most powerful Power go and collect every other power, and Kill reveals that the player is Speak.

The player runs through difficult platforming challenges using all the powers that they got, before they get trapped and Kill takes them over. They enter into the mind of the player, where the player will do a final battle with Kill, both of them using every power. Once the player has them defeated, a dialogue box will appear, saying "Press [key] to kill"

Kill "You've won [player]. Do it. Kill me. Defeat the villain."

Player doesn't yet.

Kill "What are you waiting for?"

Kill will continue saying things like this.

If they wait, then other options will appear. One for each path, whichever path you choose.
Each path is unique, and determines how the ending goes.

# Act 10: Return
You must go back through the game, which changes depending on how you chose to respond to Kill's request. 

After returning home, the Player comes back at night, a different person. Their friends greet them on the way home, but it isn't the same.
