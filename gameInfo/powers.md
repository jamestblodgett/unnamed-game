# Rush
Momentum
### Power / Afterpowr
Player can dash forward, making giant leaps and traveling large distances quickly.

Player speed perma-increase, dash blocks damage.
### Ideal
Move forward, leave behind your problems and competition.
Act before anything has the chance to stop you.
### Final Choice - Run
You leave Kill - and your mind. You flee through Kill's abandoned resting place, leaving. Kill lied, you need to ask the Bearers how to actually leave this place.
Kill will not follow, but upon trying to leave, you will realize that you do truly have to confront Kill, one way or another.
### Interactions
NPC's will try to delay you, but you just want to get away from Kill, and to get home. NPC's will be seen as delays.

# Phase
Evasion
### Power / Afterpower
Phase through things, protecting you breifly from attacks and going through enemies without taking a hit.

Phase now a toggle on-off, and asks for confirmation before turning off.
### Ideal
Be where harm can't reach you. If conflict never finds you, you'll never have conflict.
### Final Choice - Hide
You run, but hide. Trying to outrun Kill can't be possible, but if you *hide*, then maybe it will work?
You try to phase and hide the entire time, but it's no use. No one can interact with you, and you don't know how to get home without help.
Kill finds you, and forces you to confront him.
### Interactions
NPC's will not be able to speak with you, and you won't be able to interact with anyone until you reveal yourself, whereupon Kill will strike.
